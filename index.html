How It Works

Our team at Nudesse design and create scented candles inspired by seasonal flavours and the latest scent trends, which get shipped out to our subscribers every month. Our subscribers also receive an 'About Me' card to explain the making behind the scent, along with a free gift. Our candles are wrapped, packed and shipped out on the 1st of every month.

Pricing

Our subscription costs $25 a month plus shipping ($4.99).

FAQ

  • Can I cancel at any time?

  • Do you offer a free trial?
